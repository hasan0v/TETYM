# Project Brief: BSU Student Scientific-Technical Creativity Center (SSTCC) Website

## 1. Project Vision & Goals

**Project Vision:** To develop a bilingual (Azerbaijani/English) website for the BSU Student Scientific-Technical Creativity Center (SSTCC). The site will serve as a dynamic platform to showcase the center's innovations, achievements, student ideas, and activities. It aims to foster a spirit of innovation and provide a central hub for information and collaboration.

**Key Goals:**
*   Develop a modern, futuristic, and engaging website that reflects the innovative nature of the SSTCC.
*   Effectively showcase student innovations, projects, and creative ideas.
*   Highlight the Center's awards, medals, and significant achievements.
*   Promote the various clubs and activities offered by the Center.
*   Actively solicit and encourage students to submit their innovative ideas for evaluation and potential support.
*   Clearly outline the process for idea submission, evaluation criteria, and the support provided for selected ideas.
*   Attract new students, potential collaborators, and industry partners.
*   Serve as a comprehensive information hub for current members, BSU faculty, and the general public.
*   Implement bilingual functionality (Azerbaijani/English) with automatic translation for new content.
*   Utilize Supabase for a robust and scalable backend infrastructure.
